Game Collections

Sea of Stars

Sea of Stars is a sci-fi RPG that takes place in a distant future where humanity has colonized other planets. The game follows the story of a group of space explorers who discover a mysterious alien artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world western adventure game set in the late 1800s. The game follows the story of Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, as they try to survive in a rapidly changing American West.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game set in the Titanfall universe. The game features a variety of playable characters, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles.

Ace Attorney

Ace Attorney is a visual novel adventure game series that follows the story of Phoenix Wright, a defense attorney who must investigate crimes and defend his clients in court.

COD BlackOps

Call of Duty: Black Ops is a first-person shooter game that follows the story of a CIA operative who must stop a Soviet plot to destroy the United States.

Dead by Daylight

Dead by Daylight is a multiplayer survival horror game where one player takes on the role of a killer, while the other players play as survivors who must work together to escape.
